OVERVIEW

" Unlike traditional, passive expense trackers that merely log past transactions, Arbor Finance acts as an active financial co-pilot. It leverages automated transaction tracking, conversational artificial intelligence, and proactive predictive insights to help users optimize their spending, master customized budgeting goals, and accelerate their long-term savings strategies through a single, cohesive ecosystem. "

OUR APPROACH

our Understanding

CHALLENGES

The primary challenge lies in bridging the gap between passive tracking and proactive AI guidance without overwhelming the user. We must ensure the AI’s predictive insights are accurate to build trust, handle complex financial data privacy securely, and design a conversational interface that feels helpful rather than intrusive. Additionally, the system must adapt to varying financial behaviors—from rigid savers to fluctuating freelance earners—ensuring the user experience remains seamless across different real-world economic conditions.

TARGET

The target users are (1) young professionals and early-career individuals looking to automate their savings; (2) freelancers and gig-economy workers who deal with irregular income flows; and (3) budget-conscious individuals who feel overwhelmed by standard accounting and need a "co-pilot" to simplify financial decision-making. Additionally, it serves (4) users interested in long-term wealth building who require predictive modeling to optimize their capital allocation.

MY DELIVERABLE

as a UX/ UI designer

USER RESEARCH

"After defining the core mission of Arbor Finance, I conducted a series of user research sessions with focus groups ranging from finance novices to active retail investors."


RESEARCH STATEMENT PROBLEM : How do users currently experience "financial guilt" or anxiety, and what triggers cause them to abandon manual expense tracking apps?


HYPHOTHESIS : By shifting the user interface from a retrospective reporting tool (showing past errors) to a proactive AI co-pilot (offering actionable, future-looking advice), we can reduce user anxiety and increase consistent engagement.


OBJECTIVE : To uncover the specific pain points where manual data entry causes friction and to test user tolerance for AI-driven "nudges" regarding spending limits.
