OUR APPROACH

our Understanding

CHALLENGES

The challenges in commercial mango quality classification include accurately distinguishing between the subtle differences in appearance, texture, and ripeness of each variety, especially under varying environmental conditions. Additionally, implementing machine learning models that can consistently perform across different mango batches and real-world settings poses a significant challenge in terms of data variability and model generalization.

TARGET

The target of this project is to develop an automated system that can accurately classify and grade mangoes based on their variety and quality, ensuring consistency and reliability in the selection process. This system aims to improve efficiency in the supply chain, reduce human error, and enhance the overall consumer experience by delivering high-quality mangoes.
