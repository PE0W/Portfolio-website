TOOLS

DATE

Jan 12, 2025 - Feb 10, 2025

OVERVIEW

"A responsive web platform was designed using Figma to streamline the online tutoring experience on yourExamtutor.com. The interface supports both students booking lessons and tutors applying to teach. The design includes a comprehensive booking system with subject/topic selection, scheduling, and tutor matching, as well as dashboards for tracking progress and session history. The layout emphasizes intuitive navigation, academic clarity, and accessibility across devices. Key sections such as “Book Your Class,” “Become a Tutor,” and “Resources” are designed with user flow and conversion in mind, while the color palette reinforces a calm and credible educational tone."

OUR APPROACH

our Understanding

CHALLENGES

Designing a dual-sided platform for students and tutors required balancing functionality with simplicity. Key challenges included making the booking process efficient without overwhelming first-time users, ensuring that the tutor application process was trustworthy and user-friendly, and maintaining academic professionalism throughout the user interface. The platform also needed to address responsiveness, accessibility, and scalability while being visually engaging and aligned with the brand’s tone.

TARGET

The platform targets (1) secondary school students preparing for IGCSE, AS, and A-Level exams who require flexible, high-quality academic support, and (2) parents looking for vetted, convenient tutoring options online. Additionally, it is aimed at (3) qualified teachers and subject experts seeking to become tutors, (4) self-directed learners in need of organized learning resources, and (5) tech-literate users who value streamlined interfaces and reliable progress tracking when choosing educational platforms.
