OVERVIEW

"Sport Complex Management is responsible for overseeing the daily operations of the facility, including managing staff work schedules and workload. By efficiently planning shifts and tasks, management ensures that all areas of the complex are properly staffed and operational during peak and off-peak hours. This approach helps optimize resources, maintain high service standards, and adapt to varying demands, ensuring smooth operations and a positive experience for customers."

OUR APPROACH

our Understanding

CHALLENGES

Managing a sport complex include balancing staff schedules to ensure adequate coverage during peak and off-peak times while avoiding overworking staff. Additionally, coordinating staff for special events or sudden increases in demand can be difficult, requiring quick adjustments. Ensuring that all staff members are properly trained and effectively.

TARGET

The target for this project in a university sport complex is to effectively manage the scheduling and workload of staff, including athletic trainers, facility attendants, and event coordinators. The goal is to ensure optimal staffing for daily operations, peak usage times, and special events, while maintaining high levels of service and supporting the university community's recreational and athletic needs. The target users include both staff members who work in the facility and students, faculty, and staff who utilize the sports complex for fitness, recreation, and sporting events.
